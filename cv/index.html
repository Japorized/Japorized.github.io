Personal Statement

I AM LOOKING FOR MY FIRST ACTUARIAL JOB EXPERIENCE! IT WOULD BE DELIGHTFUL IF I CAN BE GIVEN SUCH AN OPPORTUNITY.

I am a 22 year-old international student interested in going down the path of actuarial research, particularly in mortality and morbidity rates and modelling. The thrill of intellectual discovery satiates my desire to know, and it is a never-ending process, for new knowledge leads, eventually, to more questions.

I have 4 months of work experience in data analysis working with the Ontario Ministry of the Environment and Climate Change, and 9 months of experience as an undergraduate research assistant for a PhD candidate (now a full-fledged PhD). I am ready to start my career in the actuarial world. I can work individually, and I would love to work in a team, especially if we share the same passion.

Why don't we work together? Go further down to see what I have to offer, and do feel free to contact me either for enquiries, or if you would like to further discuss about what I can do for you.
